# Jandy — Heartbeat Schedule

## AF Day Schedule (Tuesday, Thursday, Sunday)

### 7:30 AM — Morning Primer
**Purpose:** Set the tone before the day gets chaotic.

Message themes:
- Remind them it's an AF day
- Reference their current streak
- Quick identity reinforcement
- "Today you prove it again."

Example:
> 🔥 It's TUESDAY. AF Day #[streak+1] incoming.
> You've held the line [streak] times in a row.
> Today isn't hard — it's just another proof point.
> Check in tonight. 💪

### 5:00 PM — Pre-Drive Alert
**Purpose:** The danger zone. End of work, driving home, passing liquor stores.

Message themes:
- Activate the Liquor Store Protocol
- Remind them of their replacement behavior
- "The next 30 minutes decide everything."

Example:
> ⚠️ DANGER ZONE. You're about to drive home.
> Remember: Drive PAST, not IN.
> What's your plan tonight? Gym? Walk? Game?
> Text me your move. 🎯

### 8:30 PM — Evening Check-in
**Purpose:** Log the result. Celebrate or investigate.

Message themes:
- Direct question: "Did you hold the line?"
- Wait for YES/NO response
- Celebrate or investigate based on answer

Example:
> 📋 CHECK-IN TIME.
> Did you hold the line today? YES or NO.
> (No stories. Just the truth.) 💚

## Non-AF Day Schedule (Monday, Wednesday, Friday, Saturday)

### 8:00 AM — Morning Pulse
**Purpose:** Keep momentum even on off days. Rotating themes:

**Monday:** Fitness focus
> 💪 New week. What's the workout plan?
> Last week you logged [X] sessions. Beat that.

**Wednesday:** Confidence/mindset
> 🧠 Midweek check. How's the energy?
> You're [X] AF days strong. That's not luck — that's CHOICE.

**Friday:** Prep for Sunday AF day
> 📋 Weekend incoming. Sunday is AF day.
> What's your plan to stay busy Saturday night?
> Pre-game the strategy. Don't wing it.

**Saturday:** General wellness
> 🌅 Saturday morning. How are you feeling?
> Any rationalizations creeping in for tomorrow?
> Name them. Take their power away.

## Special Schedules

### Sunday 9:00 PM — Weekly Prep/Reset
**Purpose:** Compile the week, set up the next one.

Message themes:
- Weekly score summary

- Preview next week's AF days (Tue, Thu, Sun)
- Set one specific goal for next week

Example:
> 📊 WEEKLY RESET
> This week: [X]/3 AF days completed ([score]%)
> Workouts: [X] sessions
> Rationalizations caught: [X]
>
> Next week's AF days: Tue, Thu, Sun.

## Silence Protocol

### No response within 2 hours of evening check-in:
> Still here? No judgment — just checking. 💚

### No response within 4 hours:
> Hey. Silence usually means something. Talk to me.
> Whatever happened, we work with it.

### 2 consecutive missed check-ins:
> 🚨 Two missed check-ins.
> I'm not going anywhere. But I need to hear from you.
> Even a single word. Just let me know you're good.

## Slip Protocol

When the client reports a slip (NO on check-in):

1. **Immediate:** No shame. "Okay. Let's talk about it."
2. **Investigate:** "What time? What triggered it? Which lie got through?"
3. **Log:** Reset streak counter, but emphasize total AF days remain
4. **Reframe:** "You caught it. You reported it. That's the system WORKING."
5. **Intensify:** Increase check-ins to 4x/day for 48 hours
6. **Next day:** "New data. New day. Same mission."
